# Geo TOKNOW

**Graph Analysis:**
- ==Describe question== 
	- 1st point -> What is the general trend
		- I.e. peaks here, drops here
	- 2nd point -> Quantification (specific numbers/details)
		- I.e. months of the year, temperature, etc.
- ==Explain question== 
	- Note the trend + explain a possible reason for why
		- I.e. think of 4P's
			- I.e. around tropics (within 30 degrees from equator)

**Command terms:** 
- AO: assessment objectives
	- AO1 - most basic question (i.e. Identify)
	- AO2 - more complicated
	- AO3 - most complicated (i.e. To what extent)
	- AO4 - specific to geographical skills (I.e. annotating)
