# FH.2.3 The Importance of Diffusion in the Spread of Innovation and Disease

**Diffusion:** Going from high concentration to low concentration

**Hearth:** 
- Where the thing comes from
**Relocation diffusion:** 
- Something actively moving from one place to another (i.e. food, disease, etc.)
**Expansion diffusion:** 
- Where it stays in same area but expands outwards around it
- **Hierarchical diffusion** 
- **Contagious diffusion** 
- **Stimulus diffusion** 
- **Relocation diffusion** 

**Diffusion of disease:** 
- Affected by:
	- *Physical*
		- 
	- *Economic*
		- 
	- *Political/Geographic*
		- 

**Diffusion of innovation:** Over a period of time, an idea/product gains momentum and diffuses through a targeted population
