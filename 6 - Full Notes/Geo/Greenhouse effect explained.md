# Greenhouse effect explained

**Greenhouse Effect:** The effect by which the surface of the Earth is warmed by greenhouse gases absorbing and re-radiating IR radiation.
- **GH gasses:** Gasses like carbon dioxide, methane and nitrous oxide absorb IR radiation emitted from the Earth
	- IR radiation emitted in all directions -> temp rises
