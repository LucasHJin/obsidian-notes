# RL - Epsilon Greedy

**Epsilon greedy strategy:** policy to choose an action while balancing exploration and exploitation
- Performs exploitation $1 - \epsilon$ percent of the time and exploration $\epsilon$ percent of the time
	- Start with epsilon = 1
	- Over time reduce epsilon so more exploitation of existing knowledge and less searching
