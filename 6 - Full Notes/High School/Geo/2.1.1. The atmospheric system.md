# 2.1.1. The atmospheric system

**Atmosphere:** The envelope of gas that surrounds our planet (trapped by gravity)
- Presence of a gas
	- Ends at Karman line - 100km (arbitrary definition)
- **Benefits:**
	- Keeps us warm
	- Protects from radiation
	- Distributes heats
	- Enables breathing
- **Components:**
	- Nitrogen + oxygen ($N_{2}, O_2$)

**Layers:** (big 3)
- **Troposphere**
	- Where temperatures decrease with altitude
		- 70% of mass of atmosphere
	- Up to ~10km (minimum of 50-60C)
	- Increase up till 50km and then decrease again
- **Stratosphere**
	- 10km - 50km
		- Increases in temperature
- **Mesosphere**
	- 50km - 80km
		- Average temperature can shoot up by just ionizing a few atmos
- **Thermosphere**
	- 80km - 700km
