# m135 - Proving [[m135 - Implications|Implications]]

**General method:**
- To prove $A\implies B$:
	- Assume that hypothesis $A$ is true and use that assumption to prove $B$ 
- To prove the universally quantified implication $\forall x\in S, P(x)\implies Q(x)$ 
	- Let $x$ be an arbitrary value in $S$ and assume $P(x)$ to prove $Q(x)$ 
	- *Note:* if the hypothesis is $\forall x\in S, P(x)$ â†’ you can substitute in 1+ value of $x$ from $S$ into $P(x)$ to prove the desired conclusion
