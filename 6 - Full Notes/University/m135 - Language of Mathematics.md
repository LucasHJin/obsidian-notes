# m135 - Language of Mathematics

**Logical operator:** a symbol or keyword used to combine or compare boolean values
- I.e. $\neg$ 
**Logical expression:** a full statement that evaluates to a boolean value and often uses one or more logical operators
- I.e. $\neg A$
