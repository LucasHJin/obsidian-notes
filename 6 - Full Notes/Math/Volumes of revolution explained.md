# Volumes of revolution explained

**What is a volume of revolution?**
- Revolving (turning) a function around another function or axis to create a 3d solid
	- I.e. Creates the z-axis and makes a circular 3d object
- Can find the volume of that revolution with calculus:
	- **[[VOR - X Axis]]** 
	- **[[VOR - Y Axis]]** 
		- THESE ARE BOTH RINGS:
	- https://tutorial.math.lamar.edu/Classes/CalcI/IntAppsIntro.aspx
		- Rings -> normal
			- Either 1 or 2 rotated around x or y axis
