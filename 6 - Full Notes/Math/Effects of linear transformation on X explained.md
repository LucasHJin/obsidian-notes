# Effects of linear transformation on X explained

**Will affect the mean and variance:** 
- The changes made are $\cdot a$, $+b$ 
	- ${\rm{E}}\left({aX + b} \right) = a{\rm{E}}\left(X \right) + b$ 
	- ${\rm{Var}}\left({aX + b} \right) = {a^2}{\rm{Var}}\left(X \right)$ 
		- Note that adding $b$ has no effect
