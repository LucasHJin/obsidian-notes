# VOR - X Axis

**How?**
- Pretend Riemann sum / Definite Integral with respect to x is the radius
	- Because at any one point on the curve, the distance from the curve to the x axis is the radius if you imagine a full 360 degree rotation

**If x-axis:**
- ==Conditions:==
	- Let $f$ be a continuous function on the interval $[a,b]$ 
	- Let $f(x)\geq 0$ for $a<x<b$ 
	- Let $R$ be the region bounded by the $x$-axis, graph of $f$ and the lines $x=a$, $x=b$ 
		- (Area under the curve)
