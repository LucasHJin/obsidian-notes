# Renewable vs Non-renewable energy explained

**Renewable:** can be replenished at the same rate that it is used up.
- I.e. solar energy, hydroelectricity, etc.

**Non-Renewable:** cannot be renewed at a rate equal to or faster than it is consumed
- I.e. I.e. enriched uranium, fossil fuels, etc.
