# Physical vs Chemical reactions explained

**Physical:** Reaction where the substance undergoes a change without changing the atomic makeup
- I.e. change in state

**Chemical:** A rearrangement of atoms occurs forming new substance(s) with different properties
- Reactants -> products
- ALL CHEMICAL REACTIONS HAVE A **CHANGE IN ENERGY**
	- ==Converted to another form== or ==transferred (system, surroundings)== 
		- Release/Absorb
	- [[Heat and Temperature explained]]
