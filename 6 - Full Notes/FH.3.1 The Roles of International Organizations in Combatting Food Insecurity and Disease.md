# FH.3.1 The Roles of International Organizations in Combatting Food Insecurity and Disease 

**FAO:** Food and Agricultural Organization
- Seeks to help every community have access to food (not just sustenance that depends on everything going right for the farmers)
	- Better production 
	- Better nutrition
	- Better environment
	- Better life
- **Food Insecurity Experience Scale (FIES):** A scale made from 8 questions that cover a range of severity for food insecurity
	- ![[Screenshot 2025-02-24 at 8.45.25 AM.png]]

**Note:** Food insecurity and disease have always been a global issue -> only in recent decades a global approach to this problem
- Many organizations are working to advance food security and reduce spread of disease:
	- **International organizations** 
		- I.e. FAO/WHO/Unicef
	- **Non-governmental organizations (NGOs)** 
		- I.e. Medecins Sans
	- **Government organizations**
		- I.e. USAid
