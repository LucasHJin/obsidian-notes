# OOP - UML Diagrams

**Unified Modelling Language (UML):** Diagram to model objects and the relationships between objects, classes, etc. 
- Has 3 parts
	- *Class Name* 
		- I.e. Pet
	- *Class Attributes* 
		- I.e. `+age : int`
		- Will have a `+` or `-` denoting [[OOP - Encapsulation|public vs private]] 
	- *Class Methods* 
		- I.e. `+isMammal(species: string)` 
		- Will have a `+` or `-` denoting [[OOP - Encapsulation|public vs private]]
